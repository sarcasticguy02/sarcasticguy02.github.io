Projects

For my capstone project, Thing & Fist, I focused on implementing many systems into the game. I created a lot of the foundational game mechanics in the first semester for most of the enemies and Thing's (one of the playable characters) gameplay abilities. I also programmed the UI and the menus throughout the game. This project was a year long project with collaboration with many other majors. Thing & Fist is available for download on Steam and Itch.io.
